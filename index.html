Why Choose Us

Built the Connemara Way

We take pride in every coop we build. Here's what makes our coops stand apart.

🌳

Sustainable Irish Timber

We source all our timber from certified sustainable Irish forests, ensuring durability and environmental responsibility.

🌊

Atlantic-Weather Tested

Designed and built in Connemara — where Atlantic storms are a way of life. Our coops handle whatever the weather throws at them.

🦊

Predator-Proof Design

Our coops feature reinforced wire mesh, secure latches, and solid construction to keep foxes, mink, and other predators out.

🔧

Easy Maintenance

Designed for practical everyday use — easy to clean, ventilate, and access. Removable floor trays make cleaning a breeze.

🚚

Free Local Delivery

We deliver and install your coop anywhere in Connacht for free. Further afield? We'll arrange delivery at a reasonable cost.

2-Year Guarantee

Every coop comes with a full 2-year guarantee on workmanship and materials. We stand behind our craftsmanship.
