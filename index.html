Our Story

Rooted in the Land

Nestled in the rugged beauty of Connemara, we're passionate about raising happy, healthy hens and crafting coops that are as beautiful as they are functional.

Free Range Living

Our hens enjoy the wild Connemara landscape, foraging freely on lush green pastures.

Heritage Breeds

We raise only the finest heritage breeds, selected for temperament, egg quality, and hardiness.

Handcrafted Coops

Every coop is built by hand from solid treated timber with a corrugated metal roof, raised on corner legs with a wooden access ramp.

The Person Behind the Passion

Alexander Matz

Founder & Head Keeper

Born and raised in the wild beauty of Connemara, Alexander has spent years perfecting the craft of raising heritage hens and building coops that stand up to the West of Ireland weather. What started as a small backyard flock grew into a passion for sharing quality birds and handcrafted coops with fellow poultry keepers across Ireland.

Why Choose Us
