From Egg to Chick

Hatch Eggs Service

Want to experience the magic of hatching your own heritage chicks? We offer fertile hatching eggs from our Connemara flock. Send us a quote and we'll get you started on your hatching journey.

The Hatching Process

From Incubator to Brooder

Our fertile eggs go straight from our healthy, free-range hens into the incubator. After 21 days of careful monitoring, fluffy chicks emerge — ready to begin life in your flock.

How It Works

Simple Steps to Your Own Chicks

1. Request a Quote

Get in touch via WhatsApp or email. Tell us how many eggs you'd like, which breeds interest you, and when you're hoping to hatch.

2. Collect Your Eggs

Once we've agreed on details, you collect fresh fertile eggs from our Connemara farm — or we arrange local delivery. Eggs are packaged carefully to protect them in transit.

3. Incubate & Hatch

Pop them in your incubator at 37.5 °C with the correct humidity. After 21 days of turning and monitoring, watch your chicks pip and hatch!
